# Scripts

Files of code regulating the different behaviors

### EnemyScripts
Regulating the different enemies (archer, chasisers, turret, dragon, goat) and damaging prefabs (Falling rocks, flames). Both for their health and their behavior

### LogicScripts
Handling events "out of scene" (collect of power, menus, do not destroy)

### MusicScripts
Regulating the sounds and music

### NPCScripts
For NPCs who only talk to the player (so, mostly regulating the dialogues)

### PlayerScripts
Scripts managing player. Their attacks, their health and damage

### TutoScripts
Scripts simplified from WorldScripts, hardcoded for the simple case of the tutorial (3 rooms one above the other)

### WorldScripts
Handle the changing of scene, the construction and update of the minimap
